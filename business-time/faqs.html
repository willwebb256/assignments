GoTrip - Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How much does it cost? A: The cost is set by the host.
  • Q: What if I need to cancel? A: You can cancel anytime up to 24hrs in advance.
  • Q: How can I trust the guide? A: Every guide is certified in Outdoor Emergency Care, and goes through an exstensive background check and vetting period.
  • Q: What if I get injured? A: You must sign a limited liability waiver beforehand.
